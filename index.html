While I do have some material targets set that I would want to achieve, my true goal is making a positive impact. No matter how big or small that impact is, whatever I do I want to do in a way that will make someone's life better in some aspect. I believe it is this experience that will offer me the opportunities to engage and learn a lot more, eventually growing as a person.

Even if perfection is not possible, I think there are some things I am quite proficient at. After spending countless hours learning, practicing and rectifying mistakes, computer programming and problem solving ability are some of the strongest among my skill set. Additionally, I believe I have good communication skills and can convey my message in an easy yet effective manner. I have also been told I can be a really good friend - maybe you should see for yourself? :)

Having read it in Grade 6, William E. Hickson's "If at first you don't succeed, try try try again" pops up in my mind everytime I face a challenging situation. This philosophy, along with my motivation of being able to make a positive impact, inculcates a "never give up" philosophy in my mind. Thus I keep on going, overcoming challenges and learning from them no matter how difficult things may seem.

I am very fond of music and, due to this love I have for it, spend my free time learning and practicing guitar. Strumming and experimenting with the strings is my way of giving myself a break. I am also a sports enthusiast and enjoy playing football (Real Madrid fan) and cricket. Absolutely crazy about stargazing and astronomy. Apart from that, I like to keep myself updated with national and international political climate.
